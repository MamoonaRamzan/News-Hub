2) Analysis: IPCC’s seventh assessment has record-high representation from global south

Summary: The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) seventh assessment cycle (AR7) will feature a record-high representation from global south institutions, with 42% of authors based at institutions in the global south. The three working group reports, scheduled to be published by 2029, will have an equal 50-50 representation of authors who are citizens of the global north and global south. The AR7 working groups will comprise over 660 scientists from 90 countries.
